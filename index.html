Steve Jobs

1955 - 2011

↓

1955

Steve Jobs is born in San Francisco, California.

1976

Co-founds Apple with Steve Wozniak and Ronald Wayne.

1984

Steve Jobs unveils the Macintosh.

1985

Founds NeXT and helps create Pixar.

1998

Introduces the first iMac, revolutionizing personal computing.

2001

Releases the first iPod: 1000 songs in your pocket.

2007

Launches the first iPhone and changes mobile forever.

2010

Unveils the iPad, redefining tablets.

2011

Steve Jobs passes away. A visionary is remembered.
